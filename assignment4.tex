\section *{\centerline{AdMob}}
AdMob is Google's advertising platform for promoting and monetizing mobile applications
It enables app owners to make money without having to look for advertisers personally this is all done and optimized by google[1].
App advertising makes applications cheaper[2].

AdMob has a built-in mechanism that lets developers show 'house ads' to cross promote their portfolio of other apps for free. AdMob can also power your direct deals, which lets you run your own directly-negotiated ad deals with advertisers.[3]

It also allows to promote your app free across similar apps and improves in app purchases by promoting the app to users most likely to purchase the app[4]
